The Directory

People making it happen

Welcome to the Unplug Magazine Directory, a premier platform designed to showcase the diverse talents and distinguished portfolios of professionals across various industries. Our directory serves as an elegant, sophisticated space to highlight your expertise, elevate your visibility, and create meaningful exposure on a global stage.

Each individual profile can include a full biography, achievements, projects, career highlights, a personal quote, social media handles, a photo gallery, linked videos, and contact details — depending on the package selected.

Ready to elevate your profile? Explore our directory today, find new opportunities, and make your mark in the world.

Once-off pricing

Directory Packages

Basic

R150 once-off

Entry-level listing — name, category, short bio, and one contact method.

Pro

R280 once-off

Full biography, achievements, career highlights, quote, and social handles.

Premium

R400 once-off

Everything in Pro, plus a full project gallery and linked videos.

Want to move up a tier later? Upgrades are a flat R250 admin fee — no need to pay the price difference. Downgrading to a lower package isn't available once you're listed.
